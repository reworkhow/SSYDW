Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

How do I get started?

如何开始？

Download the SSYDW app from the App Store, create an account, and add your pet(s). If you're an existing SSYDW client, use the email associated with your account.

从 App Store 下载 SSYDW 应用，创建账户并添加您的宠物。如果您是现有客户，请使用与您账户关联的电子邮件。

How do I log a daily health check?

如何记录每日健康检查？

Tap the + button, then select "Quick Health Check." Record appetite, energy level, and any symptoms or notes.

点击 + 按钮，选择「快速健康检查」。记录食欲、精神状态和任何症状或备注。

How do I message my care team?

如何联系护理团队？

Go to the Messages tab. Use this for non-urgent questions. For emergencies, call the clinic directly.

前往消息标签页。用于非紧急问题。紧急情况请直接致电诊所。

I forgot my password. What do I do?

忘记密码怎么办？

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

在登录界面点击「忘记密码？」，输入您的电子邮件，您将收到重置链接。

Not for emergencies. SSYDW is for routine care and non-urgent communication. For urgent pet health concerns, call your clinic directly.

非紧急用途。SSYDW 用于常规护理和非紧急沟通。紧急情况请直接致电诊所。
