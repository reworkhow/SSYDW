Privacy Policy

隐私政策

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

最后更新：2026年1月21日

Introduction

简介

SSYDW ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

SSYDW（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您在使用我们移动应用和服务时的信息。

Information We Collect

我们收集的信息

Personal Information: When you register, we collect email address, full name, and profile information.

个人信息：注册时我们收集电子邮件、全名和资料信息。

Pet Information: Pet name, species, breed, health records, care notes, and messages with your care team.

宠物信息：宠物名称、种类、品种、健康记录、护理备注及与护理团队的消息。

Camera and Photo Library: With your permission, we access these solely for pet photos used in profiles and health records.

相机和相册：经您同意，我们仅用于宠物资料和健康记录中的照片。

How We Use Your Information

我们如何使用您的信息

  • Create and manage pet profiles
  • Track pet health history and daily logs
  • Facilitate communication with your care team
  • Send health reminders and notifications (with consent)
  • Improve our services
  • 创建和管理宠物档案
  • 追踪宠物健康历史和每日记录
  • 促进与护理团队的沟通
  • 发送健康提醒和通知（经同意）
  • 改进我们的服务

Data Security

数据安全

Your data is stored with industry-standard encryption. We do NOT sell your personal information.

您的数据采用行业标准加密存储。我们不会出售您的个人信息。

Your Rights

您的权利

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to disable push notifications.

您有权访问、更正、删除或导出您的数据，并可禁用推送通知。

Contact Us

联系我们

Questions? Email support@ssydw.com. We respond within 7 business days.

有问题？请发邮件至 support@ssydw.com。我们将在7个工作日内回复。
